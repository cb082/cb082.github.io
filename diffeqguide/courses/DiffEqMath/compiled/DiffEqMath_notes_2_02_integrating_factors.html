Integrating Factors

The method of solving a first-order ODE or IVP by using an integrating factor is a

An integrating factor is a function $\mu(t)$ such that when we multiply both sides of a first-order ODE by $\mu(t)$, the left-hand side becomes the derivative of a product of functions.
